1.4 Community Challenge: Build a 30s Explainer

---

## What You'll Get From This

Something real in your portfolio. A finished animation you made with the workflow you just learned.

---

#### The Challenge

Build a 30-second animated explainer on any concept you choose.

**The specs:**

- 30 seconds (give or take 5 seconds)
- Explains one concept clearly
- Uses the script → spec → build → render workflow from this course
- Rendered to video (MP4 or MOV)

That's it. Topic is yours. Style is yours. The only requirement is that you use the workflow.

---

## Why 30 Seconds

Long enough to explain something real. Short enough to finish in one session.

If you can explain a concept in 30 seconds with visuals that support the explanation, you understand both the concept and the animation workflow. This is the test.

---

## Starter Brief Template

Copy this. Fill it in. Use it to generate your spec.

```
EXPLAINER BRIEF

CONCEPT:
[What are you explaining? One sentence.]

AUDIENCE:
[Who is this for? What do they already know?]

THE HOOK (0:00-0:05):
[How do you grab attention? What's the question or surprising fact?]

THE EXPLANATION (0:05-0:25):
[What are the 2-3 beats that explain the concept? Keep it simple.]

THE PAYOFF (0:25-0:30):
[What's the takeaway? What should they remember?]

VISUAL STYLE:
[Minimal? Bold? Playful? Dark background? Light? What colors?]

REFERENCE (optional):
[Any animations or videos that have the vibe you want?]
```

Once you fill this in, give it to Claude:

```
Here's my brief for a 30-second explainer: [paste brief]
Write a spec for this animation.
```

Then build it, preview it, edit it, render it.

---

## Ideas If You're Stuck

**Explain something from your work:**

- How your product works (30-second pitch)
- A process your team follows
- A concept you explain to clients repeatedly

**Explain something you recently learned:**

- A mental model that clicked for you
- A technical concept you finally understood
- Something from this course (meta, but it works)

**Explain something everyone gets wrong:**

- A common misconception in your field
- Why people fail at something
- The difference between two things people confuse

**Explain something simple but interesting:**

- Why the sky is blue
- How compound interest works
- What makes a joke funny

The concept doesn't need to be profound. It needs to be clear. Pick something you can explain in three beats.

---

## What to Post

When you're done, share in the check-in thread:

1. **The video** (upload or link)
2. **The concept** (one sentence on what you explained)
3. **The tools** (Remotion + what else? Any custom SVGs? Any specific skills?)
4. **What you'd do differently** (what did you learn for next time?)

That last one matters. Every build teaches you something. Share it so others learn too.

---

## Featured Builds

We'll feature standout submissions in the community. What makes a build stand out:

- **Clarity** - The concept lands in 30 seconds
- **Craft** - The animations support the explanation (not just decoration)
- **Creativity** - You did something unexpected with the workflow
- **Honesty** - You shared what went wrong and what you learned

You don't need to be a designer. You don't need perfect animations. You need to ship something and share what you learned.
