2.1 Setting Up Claude in Chrome (5 Min)

1:19

---

## What You'll Get From This

Claude installed and working in your browser, ready to interact with any page you visit.

---

## The Lesson

## What You Need Before Starting

- A paid Claude plan (Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise)
- Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge
- Your Claude account login credentials

Brave, Arc, and other Chromium browsers don't work yet. Stick with Chrome or Edge.

---

### Step 1: Install the Extension

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search "Claude" or go directly to the Anthropic publisher page. Click "Add to Chrome." Confirm when prompted.

---

### Step 2: Restart Chrome

Close Chrome completely and reopen it. This lets the native messaging host initialize properly. Skip this and you'll get connection errors later.

---

### Step 3: Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar (top right). Find Claude in the list. Click the pin icon next to it. The Claude icon now stays visible in your toolbar.

---

### Step 4: Sign In

Click the Claude icon in your toolbar. A sidebar opens. Sign in with your Claude account credentials. Same email and password you use for [claude.ai](http://claude.ai/ "http://claude.ai").

---

### Step 5: Grant Permissions

Chrome will ask you to approve permissions. Claude needs these to interact with pages on your behalf:

- **Read and change data on sites you visit** (how Claude clicks, fills forms, reads page content)
- **Access to tabs** (so Claude can work across multiple tabs)

Grant them. You can manage site-level permissions later in the extension settings.

---

### Step 6: Test the Connection

With the sidebar open, type something simple: "What page am I on?" or "Summarize this page."

If Claude responds with information about your current tab, you're connected.

---

### Step 7: Know the Safety Basics

- Start with sites you trust
- Use "Ask before acting" mode for anything sensitive
- Claude will pause and ask you to handle logins and CAPTCHAs manually
- If something feels off, click the stop button in the sidebar

---

## Troubleshooting

**"Connection error" or "Failed to connect"** Did you restart Chrome after installing? Close it completely (not just the tab) and reopen. This is the fix 90% of the time.

**Extension installed but no Claude icon** Click the puzzle piece in your toolbar. Find Claude. Click the pin icon to make it visible.

**Sidebar opens but Claude won't respond** Check that you're signed in. Click the Claude icon, look for your account email. If you see a sign-in prompt instead, you're logged out.

**"Permission denied" on certain sites** Some sites block extensions. Banking sites, corporate intranets, and certain Google pages won't work. This is intentional. Try a different site.

**Still stuck?** Drop your issue in the check-in thread below. Include your browser version and what error message you're seeing.
