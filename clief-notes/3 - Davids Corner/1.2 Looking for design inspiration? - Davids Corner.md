Looking for design inspiration?

## What you'll get from this

There are many of us coming from all professions other than web development. Yet most of us need a website. I for one am not a designer and I need help. Over time, I have create a list of design resources for inspiration. These can be particulary good at providing you llm a reference style so you can **BREAKOUT** of the **Vibe Coded Slop**.

Here's a curated list of the best design idea resources for visual inspiration in 2026. These are the go-to platforms used by professional designers, illustrators, and creatives for UI/UX, graphic design, branding, illustration, product design, fashion, architecture, and more.

---

## 2 — UI/UX & Digital Product Design

- [**Mobbin**](https://mobbin.com/ "https://mobbin.com/") – Best for real mobile & web app UI patterns (thousands of screenshots)
- [**UI8**](https://ui8.net/ "https://ui8.net/") – High-quality UI kits + inspiration
- [**Lapa.ninja**](https://www.lapa.ninja/ "https://www.lapa.ninja") – Beautiful landing page gallery
- [**Pageflows**](https://pageflows.com/ "https://pageflows.com/") – User flow & interaction inspiration
- [**Refero.design**](http://refero.design/ "http://Refero.design") – Real-world SaaS interface references
- [**Screenlane**](https://www.instagram.com/screenlane/ "https://www.instagram.com/screenlane/") – Daily curated mobile app designs

## 3 — Graphic Design & Illustration

- [**Abduzeedo**](https://abduzeedo.com/ "https://abduzeedo.com/") – Daily design & illustration articles + galleries
- [**It's Nice That**](https://www.itsnicethat.com/ "https://www.itsnicethat.com/") – Creative projects across disciplines
- [**Creative Bloq**](https://www.creativebloq.com/ "https://www.creativebloq.com/") – News + inspiration roundups
- [**Illustration Daily**](https://www.instagram.com/illustration_daily/ "https://www.instagram.com/illustration_daily/") / The Illustration Room
- **MIMO (Made in Moon)** – Beautiful curated design feed

## 4 — Branding & Logo Design

- [**Logopond**](https://logopond.com/ "https://logopond.com/") – Logo inspiration

## 8 — Physical & Offline Inspiration Sources

- Design annuals & books (e.g., Graphis, Communication Arts, HOW Magazine)
- Museum exhibitions (MoMA, Cooper Hewitt, V&A)
- Street photography & travel

## How to use these

Create themed moodboards on Pinterest or [Are.na](http://are.na/ "http://Are.na") or use Canva + MCP. Save "reference" folders in your design software. Follow "Year in Review" or "Best of 2025/2026" roundups on Awwwards, Behance, and Dribbble. Save to your openBrain.

## Disclaimer

This is garage tinkering — not production gospel. These resources work for me, but your mileage may vary. Duct-tape what works and break what doesn't.
