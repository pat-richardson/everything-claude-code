## DISCORD SETUP

---

**What You'll Get From This**

Discord access set up and ready. You will know which channels to pay attention to and how Discord and Skool work together.

---

**Getting Set Up**

**Step 1:** [**Join the Discord**](https://discord.gg/BeGMhFefjx "https://discord.gg/BeGMhFefjx")

Use the same name (or close to it) that you use on Skool so we can recognize you.

**Step 2: Get your role** Once you join, you should receive your Premium role automatically. If you do not see the Premium channels within a few minutes, post in the general channel or message a mod and we will get you sorted.

**Step 3: Know what is where**

Discord and Skool do different jobs:

**Skool** = Courses, long-form content, check-in discussions, the structured learning experience. This is the home base. Everything lives here.

**Discord** = Quick questions, real-time help, troubleshooting, community conversation that moves too fast for Skool posts. This is the back channel.

If a conversation in Discord would benefit other members who are not online right now, it belongs as a Skool post instead. Discord supports Skool. It is not a second community.
